04

ABY Phase Check — Do This Every Session

Make-or-Break Step OUTPUT A+Y · ISOLATION ON · PHASE = BY EAR
  1. Dial both amps + pedals to spec. ABY: Output A+Y, Isolation ON (transformer), Phase Normal.
  2. Level-match first. Solo A (Twin to 0), note loudness. Solo B. Turn the Twin DOWN to match the 40W Blues Deluxe — it is the reference.
  3. Both amps up. Hold a low open-A chord. Listen to the body / low end.
  4. Flip the ABY Phase switch. Play the exact same chord identically.
  5. Keep the position with MORE low end & fuller center. Wrong = thin, hollow, scooped. Right = blooms. If subtle → you didn't level-match, redo step 2.
  6. Mono check: stand centered between the cabs and judge from there. Hollow there but fine off-axis = phase still wrong.

Honest Note

This nails SRV's one-dirty + one-clean-fat split. It will NOT do "two cranked dirty Fenders" — the Twin is digital and never power-distorts. The asymmetric-cleanup payoff needs the Blues Deluxe genuinely pushed. ~30% of SRV's thickness is .013 strings at Eb + his right hand — no rig setting recovers that part.
