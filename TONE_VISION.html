the translation STEP 2
received: McCready · Aching
Unresolved longing.
Beautiful tension.
This feeling lives between subdominant motion and dominant pull — where a chord wants to resolve but the melody refuses to let it land.

McCready generates this by playing against the resolution — landing on the 9th, the major 7th, the 4th. Notes that are in the key but sit just outside the chord, creating the ache without breaking the harmony.

This is not pentatonic territory. The pentatonic resolves too cleanly. You need the notes it leaves out.
